# Coverage

This document describes what regulatory content is covered by the Estonian Financial Regulation MCP server.

## Authority

**Finantsinspektsioon (Estonian Financial Supervision Authority)**
- Website: https://www.fi.ee/
- Country: Estonia (EE)
- Languages: Estonian (et), English (en)

## Sourcebooks

| Sourcebook ID | Name | Language | Content Type |
|---------------|------|----------|-------------|
| `FI_Juhendid` | Guidelines (Juhendid) | et, en | Binding regulatory guidelines for financial institutions |
| `FI_Soovituslikud_Juhendid` | Recommended Guidelines (Soovituslikud Juhendid) | et, en | Non-binding best practice recommendations |
| `FI_Ringkirjad` | Circulars (Ringkirjad) | et | Supervisory circulars to regulated entities |

## Enforcement Actions

Supervisory decisions from Finantsinspektsioon including:
- Fines (trahvid)
- Activity bans (tegevuskeelud)
- Restrictions (piirangud)
- Warnings (hoiatused)

## Coverage Gaps

- Not all historical provisions may be indexed
- Estonian-language documents may lack English translations
- Coverage is updated periodically — verify recent publications directly at https://www.fi.ee/
- Enforcement actions may lag official publication dates

## Data Currency

Data is updated periodically. Use `ee_fin_check_data_freshness` to check the database age.
For authoritative and up-to-date information, always refer to the official Finantsinspektsioon website.

See also `data/coverage.json` for machine-readable coverage metadata.
